MALTESE DOG

Maltese dog refers both to an ancient variety of dwarf canine from Italy and generally associated also with the island of Malta, and to a modern breed of dog in the toy group. The contemporary variety is genetically related to the Bichon, Bolognese, and Havanese breeds. Life expectancy: 12 – 15 years Temperament: Playful, Lively, Docile, Easygoing, Intelligent, MORE Hypoallergenic: Yes Height: Male: 21–25 cm, Female: 20–23 cm Origin: Italy, Mediterranean Basin Color: White Weight: Male: 3–4 kg, Female: 3–4 kg

AFGAN HOUND DOG

The Afghan Hound is a hound that is distinguished by its thick, fine, silky coat and its tail with a ring curl at the end. The breed is selectively bred for its unique features in the cold mountains of Afghanistan. Its local name is Tāžī Spay or Sag-e Tāzī. Temperament: Dignified, Aloof, Happy, Clownish, Independent Origin: Afghanistan Height: Male: 68–74 cm, Female: 60–69 cm Life expectancy: 12 – 14 years Weight: Male: 26–34 kg, Female: 26–34 kg Hypoallergenic: Yes Colors: Black, Cream, Red

POODLE DOG

The Poodle, called the Pudel in German and the Caniche in French, is a breed of water dog. The breed is divided into four varieties based on size, the Standard Poodle, Medium Poodle, Miniature Poodle and Toy Poodle, although the Medium Poodle is not universally recognised. Height: Toy Poodle: 24 – 28 cm, Miniature Poodle: 28 – 35 cm, Standard Poodle: 45 – 60 cm, Medium Poodle: 35 – 45 cm Origin: France, Germany Hypoallergenic: Yes Colors: Black, White, Apricot, Cream, Black & White, Sable, Brown, Blue, Silver, Grey, Red Life expectancy: 12 – 15 years Temperament: Intelligent, Alert, Active, Instinctual, Faithful, Trainable Fédération Cynologique Internationale: standard

ALASKAN MALAMUTE DOG

The Alaskan Malamute is a large breed of dog that was originally bred for its strength and endurance to haul heavy freight as a sled dog and hound. It is similar to other arctic breeds such as the husky, the spitz, the Greenland Dog, Canadian Eskimo Dog, the Siberian Husky, and the Samoyed. Life expectancy: 10 – 14 years Height: Female: 56–61 cm, Male: 61–66 cm Temperament: Dignified, Devoted, Friendly, Affectionate, Loyal, Playful Weight: Female: 32–38 kg, Male: 36–43 kg Colors: Gray & White, Sable & White, Seal & White, Black & White, White & Chocolate, Red & White Origin: Alaska Hypoallergenic: No
